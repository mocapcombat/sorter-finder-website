Every night we go to sleep
and when we wake up
we eat the same breakfast;

A bowl of oatmeal,
usually with yoghurt, nuts,
maybe some coconut rasp. I like to add chia seeds and almonds. Or sweet things like banana or chocolate. Mostly always,
I put in some blueberries.

This is one of many things I repeat.
As many of us who live a repetitive life.
Some other things people like to repeat are:

laughter, talking, walking

I repeat the thoughts I have had in my head before and share them with the same people
who I have shared them with before.

Moments become clear nature to us
and they change again and become
mixed up in new natures.

We reveal ourselves to each other again and the way we come to know us is by repetition. Though, as Gertrude Stein says;

there is no such thing.
